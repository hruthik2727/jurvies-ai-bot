# 🎨 All Available UIs - Quick Reference

## 📂 You Have 4 Different Interfaces!

## 1. 🌑 Grok Style (Dark & Modern)

**File**: `grok_client.html`

### Features:
- ✅ Pure black background (#000000)
- ✅ Dark gray message bubbles
- ✅ Blue gradient for user messages
- ✅ Avatars with emojis (👤 and 🤖)
- ✅ Suggestion chips on welcome screen
- ✅ Typing indicator with animated dots
- ✅ Status badge with pulsing green dot
- ✅ Smooth animations

### Best For:
- Dark theme lovers
- Modern, sleek design
- X/Twitter style interface
- Professional look

### Colors:
- Background: Pure black
- User messages: Blue gradient
- AI messages: Dark gray
- Status: Green

## 2. 💬 ChatGPT Style (Minimal & Clean)

**File**: `chatgpt_style.html`

### Features:

- ✅ Simple header with "New chat"
- ✅ Example cards on welcome
- ✅ No sidebar, no distractions
- ✅ Clean and focused

### Best For:
- Familiar ChatGPT experience
- Minimal distractions
- Simple and elegant
- Focus on conversation

- User avatar: Purple
- AI avatar: Green
- Send button: Green
